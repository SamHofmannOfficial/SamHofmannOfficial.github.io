WHO I AM

About Me

Engineer at heart. Builder by nature.

Sam was born and raised in Georgia, where he has spent most of his life. He is currently attending High School, where he has been actively engaged in his school Robotics Team since the 6th grade.

Balancing school with his interests in engineering, technology, and creating things.

Outside of school, Sam enjoys hands-on projects, especially building and launching model rockets, experimenting with RC vehicles, and filming his projects for his YouTube channel ProjectFlight. He likes figuring out how things work, improving designs, and sharing the results with others who enjoy STEM and hobby engineering.

Growing up in Marietta has shaped many of Sam's interests, giving him the space and community to explore his creativity, test ideas, and keep pushing his projects further.

What Drives Me

I'm driven by curiosity and the satisfaction of taking something from an idea to a finished product. Whether it's calculating the thrust needed for a rocket motor or fine-tuning an RC build, the process of problem-solving is what keeps me coming back.

My goal is to study engineering at the college level and eventually work in a field where I can keep designing, building, and innovating.
