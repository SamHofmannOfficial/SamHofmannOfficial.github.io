"LOST" Mid-Power Rocket

Ongoing

This rocket is a custom-built, high-performance design centered around a 29 mm motor mount and powered by an AeroTech G80-7T composite motor. Weighing in at approximately 500 g, it delivers 28Gs of explosive thrust off the pad, accelerating rapidly to 500 mph high speed and reaching upwords of 2,000ft of altitude.
