Music & Arts

Drums, bass, guitar — from the stage to the studio

Music has been a major part of Sam's life alongside his engineering pursuits. A drummer first, with bass and electric guitar close behind, Sam has performed on stages ranging from his school chapel to the legendary Fox Theater in Atlanta. His musical journey spans rock, Christian worship, and original AI-produced Christian rock.

Chapel Band — Mount Paran Christian School

Sam has been a member of the Chapel Band at Mount Paran Christian School for about a year. He plays every other Tuesday during chapel services, rotating between drums and bass. It's been a meaningful way to combine his love for music with his faith, leading worship for the school community.
